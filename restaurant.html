Our Story

Crafted with Fire,
Served with Soul

Born from a love of bold flavors and warm hospitality, Ember & Gold has been the heartbeat of Lucknow's fine dining scene. Every dish on our menu is an exploration of heritage and innovation.

Our chefs source only the finest seasonal ingredients, transforming them over open flame into dishes that linger in memory long after the last bite.
